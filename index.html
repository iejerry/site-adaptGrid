Customizable

Through the simple tools, you can have whatever you want, wherever you need it.

Semantic

Mobile Fisrt

Responsive

For a simple two column layout, create a row and add the appropriate number of span- columns. As this is a 12-column grid, each span- spans a number of those 12 columns, and should always add up to 12 for each row (or the number of columns in the parent). 


<div class="row">
    <div class="span-1"><p>1</p></div>
    <div class="span-11"><p>11</p></div>
</div>
<div class="row">
    <div class="span-2"><p>2</p></div>
    <div class="span-10"><p>10</p></div>
</div>
<div class="row">
    <div class="span-3"><p>3</p></div>
    <div class="span-9"><p>9</p></div>
</div>

Offsetting columns

Use prefix- and suffix- to make offset, and use no-spacing to eliminate the gutter:

pre-6 6

6 suf-6

pre-1 2 suf-1

pre-1 2 suf-1

pre-1 2 suf-1

pre-2 8 suf-2

Offsets allow you to create additional space between columns in a row. Like the rest of the grid, they're nestable. You can use classes like prefix-4 and suffix-8 to manipulate your column positions in different ways. You can offset up to 11 since you wouldn't ever offset a full-width column.


<div class="row no-spacing">
    <div class="span-6 prefix-6 tablet-span-8 tablet-prefix-4 mobile-span-5 mobile-suffix-1"><p>pre-6 6</p></div>
</div>
<div class="row no-spacing">
    <div class="span-6 suffix-6 tablet-span-8 tablet-suffix-4 mobile-span-5 mobile-prefix-1"><p>6 suf-6</p></div>
</div> 
<div class="row no-spacing">
    <div class="span-2 prefix-1 suffix-1"><p>pre-1 2 suf-1</p></div>
    <div class="span-2 prefix-1 suffix-1"><p>pre-1 2 suf-1</p></div>
    <div class="span-2 prefix-1 suffix-1"><p>pre-1 2 suf-1</p></div>
</div>
<div class="row">
    <div class="span-8 prefix-2 suffix-2"><p>pre-2 8 suf-2</p></div>
</div>

Centered columns

Centered columns are placed in the middle of the row.This is accomplished by adding a class of center depending on which breakpoint you want the row to center on.

7 center

9 center

11 center


<div class="row center">
    <div class="span-7"><p>7 center</p></div>
</div>
<div class="row center">
    <div class="span-9"><p>9 center</p></div>
</div>
<div class="row center">
    <div class="span-11"><p>11 center</p></div>
</div>

With mobile classes: .mobile-half, .mobile-two-thirds, .mobile-third.
